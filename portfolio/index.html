Interaction Design

I create detailed UI specifications for all product features to match the users expectations. I work together with Product Managers, Graphic Designers and Developers to create sketches, wireframes, flow diagrams, etc to help teams understand the designs and assist them during implementation.

User Experience

An Ethnographic study of the Out Of Box Experience of a new mobile phone to understand the 1st hour/1st day/1st week and 1st month customer experience of using a new mobile phone. In this project I visited the users at their home and observed their phone setup process. Followed up on the users experiences for a one month period and moderated focus group sessions.

Worked together with two remote researchers who conducted the same project in UK and Greece at the same time.

Intranet Usability

Interaction Designer, Usability Specialist

Optimizing a corporate Intranet. The new Intranet should stimulate the ease of navigation, information retrieval and ensure a smooth online experience within and across different segment of users.

In this project I was responsible for Customer Research, Requirements Gathering, Wireframe and Prototype design and development. Worked together with a colleague to create and conduct the surveys and interviews. I created the Wireframes and Prototypes, each iteration was reviewed by an expert.

Prototyping

To keep up with all the new web technology I took the opportunity to create a small game at the Beijing TechCrunch Hackaton. This game helps students to practice a language. Ten players answer questions in real-time, and the top 3 will advance to the next level.

This prototype is build on the Node.js framework, and uses the Foundation framework so it can be played on any device (Desktop and Mobile).
